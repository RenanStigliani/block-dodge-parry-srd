### HOWLING HALLS of WHISPERSTONE

Howling Halls of Whisperstone is a compact, high-stakes dungeon delve that subverts the standard “kill-them-all” dungeoneering trope. Designed to be played in one or two sessions, it forces players to balance their tactical aggression against a looming narrative threat.
